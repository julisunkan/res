How It Works

Our platform is powered by state-of-the-art large language models that analyze your resume content alongside job descriptions to identify gaps, suggest improvements, and generate tailored application materials.

All AI processing is done through secure API connections. Your resume content is used solely to power the features you use — we don't train our models on your personal data or share it with third parties for purposes other than providing the service.
