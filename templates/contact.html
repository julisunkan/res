Frequently Asked Questions

Is my resume data private and secure?
Yes. Your resume content is stored securely and used only to provide our AI-powered services. We do not sell your personal resume data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual resumes and job applications directly from the app. To request full data deletion, use the contact form above and select "Privacy Request".
Why do I see ads on the website?
We use Google AdSense to display advertisements, which helps us keep the service free. You can opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.
How accurate is the AI-generated content?
Our AI provides a strong starting point for resumes and cover letters, but we always recommend reviewing and personalizing the output before submitting to employers. The AI is a tool to assist you, not replace your judgment.
