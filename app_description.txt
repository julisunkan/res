────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  SHORT DESCRIPTION  (suitable for app stores, directories & landing pages)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

AI Resume & Cover Letter Creator is the all-in-one AI-powered career platform
that helps job seekers land more interviews and offers — faster. Powered by
cutting-edge large language models, the app transforms the entire job search
process: upload your existing resume and get it rewritten and scored in seconds,
generate tailored cover letters with a single click, track every application in
one dashboard, practice interview questions curated for your exact role, and
optimize your LinkedIn profile to attract recruiters. Whether you are a recent
graduate or a seasoned professional, our platform gives you a competitive edge
at every stage of your career journey.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  LONG DESCRIPTION  (suitable for website About pages, press kits & pitches)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

In today's competitive job market, standing out is no longer optional — it is
essential. AI Resume & Cover Letter Creator is a next-generation career
intelligence platform designed to put the power of artificial intelligence
directly in the hands of every job seeker.

From the moment you upload your resume, our AI engine gets to work. It performs
a deep analysis of your document, identifies gaps, surfaces keyword
opportunities, scores your resume against industry benchmarks, and rewrites
every section to be sharper, more impactful, and tailored to the roles you want.
No more staring at a blank page. No more generic cover letters that get ignored.
With one click, the platform generates a compelling, personalised cover letter
that speaks directly to the job description — every single time.

Our smart Job Tracker keeps every application organised in one place. Log
companies, positions, and statuses, add private notes, and get an instant
overview of your pipeline. Never lose track of where you stand with any employer
again.

Preparing for interviews has never been more targeted. Our AI Interview Prep
feature generates role-specific questions based on the actual job description and
provides model answers so you can walk into every interview with confidence.

Struggling to get noticed on LinkedIn? Our LinkedIn Profile Optimizer analyses
your profile content and rewrites your headline, About section, and experience
bullets using recruiter-friendly language and high-impact keywords — turning your
profile into a magnet for opportunity.

And when you need real-time guidance, our Career Chat AI is always available —
a knowledgeable career advisor ready to discuss salary negotiation, career
pivots, job search strategies, or anything else on your mind, available around
the clock.

Built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), AI Resume & Cover Letter Creator works
seamlessly on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile — with no app store
download required. It is fast, responsive, and designed to meet job seekers
exactly where they are.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  CORE FEATURES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  1. AI RESUME BUILDER & OPTIMIZER
     • Upload existing resumes in PDF or DOCX format
     • Instant AI-powered resume analysis with a match score
     • Section-by-section AI rewriting (summary, experience, skills, education)
     • Keyword gap analysis against target job descriptions
     • Student Mode — generate a complete resume from skills alone (no experience
       needed)
     • Export finished resumes as polished PDF or DOCX files

  2. AI COVER LETTER GENERATOR
     • One-click personalised cover letters tailored to any job description
     • Adapts tone and content to match the company and role
     • Editable output — refine and download instantly

  3. JOB APPLICATION TRACKER
     • Centralised dashboard to log and manage every application
     • Status tracking: Applied → Interview → Offer → Rejected
     • Private notes per application
     • Real-time analytics: total applications, interview rate, offer rate
     • Application overview stats with growth tracking

  4. AI INTERVIEW PREPARATION
     • Generates role-specific interview questions from any job description
     • Provides detailed model answers for every question
     • Covers behavioural, technical, and situational question types
     • Helps candidates prepare faster and perform better

  5. LINKEDIN PROFILE OPTIMIZER
     • AI-powered rewrite of headline, About section, and experience bullets
     • Keyword optimisation for LinkedIn search and recruiter visibility
     • Actionable recommendations to improve profile completeness and appeal

  6. AI CAREER CHAT ADVISOR
     • Real-time conversational AI for career coaching and job search guidance
     • Topics include: salary negotiation, career pivots, interview tips,
       workplace challenges, industry trends, and more
     • Available 24/7 — like having a personal career coach on demand

  7. JOB BOARD
     • Curated job listings aggregated from multiple external sources
     • Featured job widget on the dashboard for instant discovery
     • Admin-managed job posts with AI-assisted rewriting tools

  8. ADMIN PANEL
     • Full-featured admin dashboard for site owners and operators
     • Manage resumes, job applications, settings, and branding
     • Configure AI model, API keys, and performance limits
     • Control footer visibility, SEO metadata, social media links, and ads
     • Google AdSense integration with placement controls
     • Google Analytics support
     • Export / import database (SQLite & MySQL compatible)
     • Switch between SQLite and MySQL without code changes

  9. PROGRESSIVE WEB APP (PWA)
     • Installable on any device — works like a native app
     • Responsive design optimised for desktop, tablet, and mobile
     • Service worker support for fast load times

  10. SECURITY & CUSTOMISATION
      • Password-protected admin panel
      • Configurable branding: app name, tagline, colours, and logo
      • Cookie consent management built in
      • Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Cookie Policy pages included
